Apple Crisp

Description

A simple dessert that's great served with ice cream.

Ingredients

  1. 10 cups all-purpose apples, peeled, cored and sliced
  2. 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  3. 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  4. ½ cup water
  5. 1 cup packed brown sugar
  6. ¼ teaspoon baking soda

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degree C).
  2. Place the sliced apples in a 9x13 inch pan. Mix the white sugar, 1 tablespoon flour and ground cinnamon together, and sprinkle over apples.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 45 minutes.
  4. Place the sliced apples in a 9x13 inch pan. Mix the white sugar, 1 tablespoon flour and ground cinnamon together, and sprinkle over apples. Pour water evenly over all.
